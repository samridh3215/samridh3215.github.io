## Overview

This project leverages blockchain technology to revolutionize food supply chain management by creating a transparent, immutable record of food products from origin to consumer.

## Objectives

- **Transparency**: Provide end-to-end visibility of food products throughout the supply chain
- **Traceability**: Enable rapid identification of contamination sources during food safety incidents
- **Decentralization**: Eliminate single points of failure and reduce intermediary dependencies
- **Trust**: Build consumer confidence through verifiable product authenticity

## Benefits

- Enhanced food safety through rapid contamination tracking
- Reduced fraud and counterfeit products
- Improved supply chain efficiency
- Greater accountability among supply chain participants
- Real-time data sharing among stakeholders
